Rockmylight

Our software is cross platform. In addition to an Android app, it runs on any device with a browser. We use state of the art supervised learning, classification techniques to extract features from audio and map it to a light wavelength. Our software gives the ability to a user say a DJ or a social media evangelist to synchronise devices with the least amount of latency or even create patterns in space and time. We can trigger events based on Geolocation. With a little bit more work, the software can be adapted to work with indoor triangulation.
